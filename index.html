GELATIN ICE SALAD



â‚±99

Ingredients

  • 25g Strawberry Flavored Gelatin
  • 4 cup boiling water
  • 4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 cup pineapple juice
  • 2cans of Alaska Evaporated Milk
  • 2cans of Alaska Condensed Milk
  • 1 cup chopped fresh pineapple
  • 500g Fruit Cocktail
  • 25g Marsmallow
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Equipments

  • Saucepan
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Serving spoons
  • Whisk
  • Refrigerator
  • Stove
  • Molds of your choice
  • Bowl
  • Knife
  • Cutting Board

Procedures

  1. Prepare all the ingredients
  2. In a waterpot, put four(4) cups of water then put it in a stove and wait for it to boil.
  3. If the water already boiled, put four(4) sachet of Strawberry Flavored Gelatin and stir it for a seconds.
  4. Place it into a gelatin mold(e.g., cubed-shaped) and wait to cool.
  5. Put the cubed-shaped gulaman in a bowl, add the Alaska Condensed and Alaska Evaporated Milk.
  6. Pour a 4 cup of fresh pineapple juice and a 1 cup of chopped fresh pineapple, after that add the 500g Fruit cocktail.
  7. Add 4 tablespoon of granulated sugar based on your sense of taste
  8. Leave it on a Refrigeratorfor a meantime and let it cool.
  9. Move it to the serving bowl/plate and you can taste the Gelatin Ice Salad.
  10. Add some marsmallow and strawberries (optional).
